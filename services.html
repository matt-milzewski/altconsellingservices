Child Protection Support

I specialize in supporting people who are involved in child protection matters and are working with child safety services. I can offer support and services to help navigate and understand the child protection system.

  • Support for parents in contact with Child Safety
  • Reunification counselling
  • Referrals welcome from Child Safety Officers

NDIS Support

NDIS Participants Welcome

I provide counselling services to self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants. If you're accessing funding under the Improved Daily Living or CB Daily Activities I can support you with wellbeing, capacity building, parenting and therapeutic support.

  • Wellbeing support
  • Capacity building
  • Parenting support
  • Therapeutic support
